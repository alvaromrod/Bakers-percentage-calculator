<p>A simple and efficient Android application for calculating baker's percentages. This app helps bakers scale recipes and understand ingredient ratios with ease.</p>

<p><b>Features:</b></p>

<ul>
<li><b>Recipe Management:</b> Create, edit, and delete recipes.</li>
<li><b>Baker's Percentage Calculation:</b> Automatically calculates ingredient weights based on flour weight and percentages.</li>
<li><b>QR Code Sharing:</b> Share recipes instantly via QR codes.</li>
<li><b>Backup &amp; Restore:</b> Save your recipes to a JSON file and restore them later.</li>
<li><b>Offline First:</b> All data is stored locally on your device.</li>
</ul>
